EDF5 Prismatic Trials

Rules

Every 72 hours a new set of challenges are generated. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to beat the stated mission in the given difficulty using the provided class and a maximum of the provided armour.

You may select any set of weapons from the provided options.

Co-Op Challenge

You and one partner can select from the classes provided, and play online. You must choose two different classes.
