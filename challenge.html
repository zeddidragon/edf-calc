EDF4.1 Prismatic Trials

Rules

Every 72 hours a new set of challenges are generated. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, is to beat the stated mission in the given difficulty using the provided class and a maximum of the provided armour.

You may select any set of weapons from the provided options, but except for Fencer no weapons your loadout should have no repeats.

Co-Op Challenge

You and 1 partner can select from the classes provided. You must play two different classes.
